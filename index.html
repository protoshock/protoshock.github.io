PROTOSHOCK is a multiplayer first-person shooter that combines the fast-paced action with movement mechanics inspired by games such as ULTRAKILL and Titanfall 2. Players take on the role of creatures known as protogens, and battle their way through a variety of maps and game modes. PROTOSHOCK features a variety of weapons and abilities that allow players to move quickly and fluidly around the maps. Players can wall run, dash, and slide, which allows them to quickly get around the map and take down their opponents. The game features a variety of maps and game modes, so players can always find something new to do. Features:

  • Multiplayer and singleplayer - While protoshock is supposed to be an multiplayer game, if the servers go down or if you just feel like it, there is always an offline mode to allow you to play with bots!
  • Custom textures - Enter in a texture URL for your protogen to customize it!
  • Mod support - Using the unity engine, you can create whatever content you wish for protoshock, whether that be a map or some other feature its your choice!
  • Vehicles - Drive around on the battlefield, or just have fun! You can also create your own vehicles using mods!
  • Gamemodes - Protoshock has multiple gamemodes to allow for replayability and allows you to try something new!
    • Gamemodes
  • Team deathmatch - Fight on a team, team with highest score wins
  • Deathmatch - Free for all, get the highest Kill/Death ratio to win
  • Plague - PVE - Fight against an increasing army of corrupted protogens, you only have 3 lives, get the highest kill/death ratio without losing your lives to win
